# Hosting Guide for Fresh Hold's

This guide provides simple steps to host your static website for free with professional features like HTTPS (SSL), custom domains, and automatic deployments.

## Recommended Hosting Providers

For a professional, fast, and free setup, we recommend **Netlify** or **Vercel**.

### Option 1: Netlify (Easiest)

1.  **Sign Up**: Go to [Netlify](https://www.netlify.com/) and create a free account.
2.  **Drag & Drop**:
    - Locate your project folder on your computer (`d:\Bhuvaneswaran\pro`).
    - Simply drag the entire folder into the Netlify "Deploy" area.
3.  **Site Live**: Your site will be live instantly with a link like `https://freshholds.netlify.app`.
4.  **Custom Domain**: In the settings, you can add your own domain like `freshholds.in`.

### Option 2: Vercel (Fast & Reliable)

1.  **Sign Up**: Go to [Vercel](https://vercel.com/) and sign up.
2.  **Import**: Connect your GitHub/GitLab account if you use one, or use the "Deploy" button to upload your project.
3.  **Deploy**: Vercel will automatically detect your HTML files and host them.

---

## Post-Deployment Checklist

1.  **Verify robots.txt**: Visit `yourdomain.com/robots.txt` to ensure search engines can crawl your site.
2.  **Verify Sitemap**: Check `yourdomain.com/sitemap.xml`.
3.  **Google Search Console**:
    - Submit your sitemap to [Google Search Console](https://search.google.com/search-console) to ensure your site starts appearing in search results.
4.  **SSL/HTTPS**: Ensure your site loads over `https://`. (Netlify/Vercel do this automatically).

## Ongoing SEO Maintenance

- **Fresh Content**: Regularly update the "Products" or "About" sections to keep search engines interested.
- **Link Building**: Share your website link on social media (Instagram, WhatsApp) to improve its ranking.
